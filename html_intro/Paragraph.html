Attributes are the characteristics or properties of an element, object,

or entity that provide additional information about it.
In various contexts, such as computer programming, data analysis, or web development, attributes play a crucial role in defining the behavior, appearance, or functionality of an element.
For instance, in HTML, attributes are used to specify the properties of an HTML element,
such as its width, height, color, or font size.
Similarly, in a database, attributes refer to the columns or fields that define the characteristics of a table or entity.
By assigning attributes to an element or object, we can provide more context, meaning, and functionality to it.
